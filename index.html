How it works

Welcome to Our GPA Calculator!

Enter your PROJECTED OBTAINED MARKS from the CU Online Portal to get started.

Our site offers four main functions:

  • Calculate GPA of Non-Lab Subjects: Enter your marks to compute your GPA for non-lab subjects.
  • Calculate GPA of Lab Subjects: Enter your marks to compute your GPA for lab subjects.
  • Marks Needed for Desired GPA (Non-Lab): Find out the marks needed in finals to achieve your target GPA for non-lab subjects.
  • Marks Needed for Desired GPA (Lab): Find out the marks needed in finals to achieve your target GPA for lab subjects.
