How it works

Welcome to Where You Stand

Your go-to platform for projecting and assessing your academic standing.

To get started, simply click on the calculate button and enter the marks based on your desired requirements.

Input the projected marks as displayed on your academic portal, and our system will provide you with valuable insights into your academic standing.

You can also check how much marks you need in finals for a particular subject to get your desired GPA

Navigate through your educational journey with ease, gaining clarity on where you stand and what it takes to achieve your goals.

Good luck on your academic endeavors!
